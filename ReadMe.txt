*THIS IS A MULTIPLAYER GAME*

*TO PLAY THIS GAME 2 PLAYERS ARE REQUIRED*

*THE INSTRUCTIONS OF THIS GAME HAS BEEN ADDED IN THE GAME ITSELF*

*ALL THE CREDITS AND THE REFERENCES HAS BEEN ADDED IN THE GAME*


					HOW TO PLAY
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* PLAYER1 = A - LEFT MOVE || W - JUMP || D - RIGHT MOVE || SPACE - SHOOT*

* PLAYER2 = LEFT ARROW - LEFT MOVE || UP ARROW - JUMP || RIGHT ARROW - RIGHT MOVE || RIGHT SHIFT - SHOOT*
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
